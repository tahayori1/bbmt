Our Products

We provide a wide range of high-quality engineered and natural wood products to meet the diverse needs of the construction industry.

MDF

Medium-density fibreboard, an engineered wood product made by breaking down wood residuals into fibres, combined with wax and resin.

HDF

High-density fiberboard (Hardboard), an engineered wood product known for its density, strength, and hardness.

PLYWOOD

Made from thermoformed plastics, UV-inhibited pigments, and foaming compounds, often derived from post-consumer waste.

HARDWOOD

Wood from dicot trees, found in broad-leaved temperate and tropical forests, known for its durability and distinct grain patterns.

CHIPBOARD

Rigid sheets from compressed wood chips and resin, often coated or veneered, used in furniture and buildings.

HPL

High-Pressure Decorative laminates used as furniture surface materials or wall paneling for a durable, stylish finish.

NATURAL VENEER

Thin slices of wood glued onto core panels to produce flat panels for doors, cabinets, and furniture.

About Bozorgi Group

INTRODUCTION

Bozorgi Building Material Company, proprietary brand of BOZORGI GROUP, started as a trading company in 1988. Operating under the motto “Business Without Boundary,“ we import, export, and distribute authorized goods worldwide.

With decades of successful experience, BOZORGI Group now focuses on supplying premium building materials, leveraging strong relationships with refineries and petrochemicals to meet customer demands globally.

OUR VISION

To be a leading business and trading company in Building Materials, maintaining our values of honesty and transparency on both national and international fronts.

OUR MISSION

To supply building materials with the best services and prices to satisfy our customers, expanding our business and geographical reach using modern international management systems.

Our History

A legacy of business excellence from 1889 to the present day. The Bozorgi family's journey in trade and innovation.

BOZORG BOZORGI

1889 - 1951: Founder of the timber business, laying the groundwork for generations to come.

ALIASGHAR BOZORGI

1909 - 1989: Started the local hometown business with timber, building on the founder's legacy.

MUHAMMADALI BOZORGI

1951 - 1990: Expanded the local business, increasing its reach and influence in the region.

ALI BOZORGI

1988 - Present: CEO at BBMTCo. A revolutionary leader who introduced global trading with hi-tech materials, making Ali Bozorgi a number one brand.

ALIASGHAR BOZORGI

2020 - now: Leading the Bozorgi Group into the future, continuing a legacy of innovation and global trade.
