Our Core Products

We supply a curated range of high-quality engineered and natural wood products for diverse construction and furnishing needs.

MDF

Engineered wood from broken down hardwood or softwood residuals, combined with wax and resin. Ideal for furniture and cabinetry.

HDF

High-Density Fiberboard, or hardboard, is an engineered wood product known for its durability and strength, used in flooring and paneling.

Polywood

A durable, eco-friendly material made from recycled plastics. Perfect for outdoor furniture due to its weather resistance.

Hardwood

Premium wood from dicot trees, valued for its beauty and longevity. Used in high-end furniture, flooring, and construction.

Chipboard

A cost-effective material made from compressed wood chips and resin, often veneered for use in furniture and buildings.

HPL

High-Pressure Laminate, a decorative surface material known for its durability and used in furniture and wall paneling.

Natural Veneer

Thin slices of real wood glued onto core panels to create beautiful surfaces for doors, cabinets, and furniture.

About Bozorgi Group

Established in 1988, Bozorgi Group operates under the motto "Business Without Boundary." We began as a global trading company and have since focused our expertise on supplying premium building materials, leveraging decades of experience and strong customer relationships.

Our competitive advantage lies in our customer-centric approach, creating win-win relationships and responding swiftly to market demands through our extensive network of suppliers and partners.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To be a leading business and trading company in Building Materials, upholding our values of honesty and transparency on a global scale.

Mission: To supply building materials with the best service and pricing, expanding our business reach through experienced management and modern business systems.

Our Legacy: A Family Tree of Business

Founder of the family's timber business, laying the foundation for generations to come.

2

3

4

A revolutionary leader who introduced high-tech materials and global trading, making the AliBozorgi brand number one.

5

Aliasghar Bozorgi (2001 - Now)

Served as partner and consultant before leading Bozorgi Group from 2020 to today.
