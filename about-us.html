ABOUT US

A Legacy of Excellence in Building Materials

Since 1988, Bozorgi Group has been a leading trading company under the motto "Business Without Boundary." With decades of successful experience, we specialize in supplying high-quality building materials, fostering strong relationships with our loyal customers worldwide.

Our competitive advantage lies in our unwavering focus on the customer, creating win-win relationships built on trust and reliability. We leverage our extensive experience and professional team to analyze market needs and deliver modern business services based on the latest international standards.

Our Vision

We wish to be one of the leading companies of business and trading in fields of Building Materials, by maintaining our values, including honesty in trade and transparency in both national and international faces.

Our Mission

The most important mission of BOZORGI group Trading Co. is to supply building materials along with offering the best services and prices to satisfy its customers in order to expand the business and the geographical range of its activities.

OUR JOURNEY

Bozorgi's Business Family Tree

From a local timber business in 1889 to a global trading powerhouse, our history is built on generations of passion and innovation.

1889 - 1951

BOZORG BOZORGI

Founder of the timber business, laying the foundation for a multi-generational legacy in material trading.

1909 - 1989

ALIASGHAR BOZORGI

Started the local hometown business with timber, continuing the family tradition.

1951 - 1990

MUHAMMADALI BOZORGI

Expanded the local business, increasing its reach and solidifying its market presence.

1988 - present

ALI BOZORGI

As CEO at BBMTCo., he introduced a revolutionary idea of global trading with hi-tech materials, transforming the business into the renowned AliBozorgi brand.

2001 - now

ALIASGHAR BOZORGI

Served as the third partner and consultant at BBMTCo before establishing the modern Bozorgi Group in 2020.
