MDF

Medium-density fibreboard is an engineered wood product made by breaking down wood residuals into fibres, combining it with wax and resin, and forming it into panels under high temperature and pressure.

HDF

High-density fiberboard, or hardboard, is a type of fiberboard, which is an engineered wood product. It is known for its density, strength, and hardness, making it ideal for flooring and furniture backing.

POLYWOOD

Made from thermoformed plastics and UV-inhibited pigments, Polywood is a durable, weather-resistant material derived from post-consumer waste like milk and detergent bottles. Perfect for outdoor applications.

Get a Quote →

HARDWOOD

Sourced from dicot trees in temperate and tropical forests. Known for its natural beauty, strength, and longevity, hardwood is a premium choice for flooring, furniture, and construction.

CHIPBOARD

Also known as particleboard, this material is made in rigid sheets from compressed wood chips and resin. It's a cost-effective solution for furniture, cabinetry, and building applications.

HPL

High-Pressure Laminates are decorative surface materials ideal for furniture and wall paneling. They offer superior durability, resistance to wear and tear, and a wide range of aesthetic options.

NATURAL VENEER

Thin slices of real wood, typically glued onto core panels to produce beautiful flat panels for doors, cabinets, and high-end furniture. It offers the authentic look of solid wood at a fraction of the cost.
