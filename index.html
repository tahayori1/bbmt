Our Products

High-quality engineered and natural wood for all your construction needs.

MDF

Medium-density fibreboard, an engineered wood product made from broken down hardwood or softwood residuals.

HDF

High-density fiberboard (Hardboard) is an engineered wood product, valued for its density and strength.

PLYWOOD

Made from thermoformed plastics, UV-inhibited pigments, and foaming compounds, often from recycled materials.

HARDWOOD

Wood from dicot trees, usually found in broad-leaved temperate and tropical forests. Known for its durability.

CHIPBOARD

Rigid sheets from compressed wood chips and resin, often coated or veneered, used in furniture and buildings.

HPL

High-Pressure Laminate products primarily used as furniture surface materials or wall paneling.

NATURAL VENEER

Thin slices of wood, usually thinner than 3 mm, that are glued onto core panels for doors, cabinets, and furniture.

About Bozorgi Group

Since 1988, Bozorgi Group has operated under the motto "Business Without Boundary." We are a trading company specializing in the import, export, and distribution of building materials worldwide.

Our Mission

To supply building materials with the best services and prices, expanding our business and geographical reach using experienced, up-to-date international management systems.

Our Vision

To be a leading business and trading company in building materials, maintaining our core values of honesty and transparency on both national and international fronts.

Company Values

Honesty, loyalty, courtesy, clarity, humility, and learning. We focus on humanity, are open to criticism, and prioritize the mutual interests of all beneficiaries.
