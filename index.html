Our Collection

Engineered for Perfection

MDF

Medium-density fibreboard, an engineered wood product made by breaking down wood residuals into fibres, combined with wax and resin.

HDF

High-density fiberboard (hardboard), is an engineered wood product known for its durability and strength.

POLYWOOD

Made from thermoformed plastics, derived from post-consumer bottle waste. A sustainable and resilient choice.

HARDWOOD

Premium wood from dicot trees, found in broad-leaved temperate and tropical forests, prized for its natural beauty and longevity.

CHIPBOARD

Rigid sheets from compressed wood chips and resin, often coated or veneered, used in furniture and buildings.

HPL

High-pressure decorative laminates used as furniture surface materials or wall paneling for a sleek, modern finish.

NATURAL VENEER

Thin slices of wood, usually thinner than 3 mm, that are glued onto core panels to produce flat panels for doors, cabinets, and furniture with an authentic wood grain finish.

Our Identity

A Legacy of Trust and Innovation

Introduction

Since 1988, Bozorgi Building Material Company has operated under the motto “Business Without Boundary“. We began as a global trading company and have since focused our expertise on supplying premium building materials. Relying on our loyal customers and a decade of successful experience, we proudly fulfill demands both regionally and internationally.

Our Vision

To be a leading company in the building materials trade, maintaining our core values of honesty and transparency on both national and international stages.

Our Mission

To supply building materials with the best services and prices, ensuring customer satisfaction and expanding our business reach through modern management systems.

Company Values

  • ✓ Honesty, loyalty, courtesy, and clarity.
  • ✓ Attention to humanity and mutual interests.
  • ✓ Openness to criticism and continuous learning.

Our Journey

A Family Tree of Business

1889 - 1951

Bozorg Bozorgi

Founder of the family's timber business, laying the groundwork for generations to come.

1909 - 1989

Aliasghar Bozorgi

Continued the legacy, starting the local hometown business with timber.

1951 - 1990

Muhammadali Bozorgi

Expanded the local business, increasing its reach and influence in the region.

1988 - Present

Ali Bozorgi

A revolutionary leader who introduced global trading and hi-tech materials, transforming the family business into the renowned AliBozorgi brand as CEO at BBMTCo.

2020 - Now

Aliasghar Bozorgi

After years as a partner and consultant at BBMTCo, he now leads the modern Bozorgi Group, steering it into the future.
