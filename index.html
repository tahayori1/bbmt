Our Products

MDF

Engineered wood product made by breaking down hardwood or softwood residuals into wood fibres.

HDF

High-density fiberboard, an engineered wood product also known as hardboard.

POLYWOOD

Made from thermoformed plastics, often derived from post-consumer bottle waste.

HARDWOOD

Wood from dicot trees, found in broad-leaved temperate and tropical forests.

CHIPBOARD

Rigid sheets from compressed wood chips and resin, used in furniture and buildings.

HPL

Decorative high-pressure laminates primarily used as furniture surface materials or wall paneling.

NATURAL VENEER

Thin slices of wood, usually thinner than 3 mm, glued onto core panels.

About Bozorgi Group

Business Without Boundary

Our Journey

Bozorgi Building Material Company, with the proprietary brand BOZORGI GROUP, has been a trading powerhouse since 1988. Starting under the motto “Business Without Boundary“, we have grown to specialize in supplying building materials worldwide. With decades of successful experience, we pride ourselves on our loyal customers and our ability to meet demands through effective relations and sourcing.

Our Vision

To be a leading company in building materials trading, maintaining our values of honesty and transparency on both national and international fronts.

Our Mission

To supply building materials with the best services and prices, expanding our business and geographical reach through modern management and business systems.
