Our Core Products

High-quality, engineered and natural wood products to meet the diverse needs of the construction industry.

MDF (Medium-Density Fibreboard)

Engineered wood from broken down hardwood or softwood residuals, combined with wax and resin for a smooth, consistent finish.

HDF (High-Density Fibreboard)

Also known as hardboard, this engineered wood product offers superior density and strength for demanding applications.

POLYWOOD

Sustainable material made from thermoformed plastics and post-consumer bottle waste. Durable and weather-resistant.

HARDWOOD

Premium wood from dicot trees found in temperate and tropical forests, known for its durability and timeless beauty.

CHIPBOARD

Rigid sheets made from compressed wood chips and resin, often coated or veneered for use in furniture and buildings.

HPL (High-Pressure Laminate)

Durable decorative laminates used as furniture surface materials or wall paneling, manufactured under high pressure.

NATURAL VENEER

Thin slices of real wood, typically glued onto core panels to produce beautiful flat panels for doors, cabinets, and furniture.
