About Bozorgi Group

A Legacy of Trust and Excellence in Trading

Our Journey

Bozorgi Group, operating under the motto "Business Without Boundary," began as a trading company in 1988. With decades of successful experience, we have focused our expertise on supplying premium building materials to a global clientele.

Our competitive advantage lies in our customer-centric approach, leveraging a deep understanding of market dynamics and a professional team to foster win-win relationships. The trust of our customers and suppliers is our greatest asset, forming a powerful chain that enables us to meet demands promptly and efficiently.

Our Vision

To be a leading business and trading company in the building materials sector, maintaining our core values of honesty and transparency on both national and international stages.

Our Mission

To supply superior building materials with exceptional service and pricing, expanding our business and geographical reach through experienced teams and modern international management systems.

Our Premium Products

A Curated Selection of High-Quality Building Materials

MDF

Medium-density fibreboard, an engineered wood product made from broken down wood residuals, wax, and resin, ideal for a wide range of applications.

HDF

High-density fiberboard (hardboard), a robust engineered wood product known for its durability and strength, suitable for demanding projects.

POLYWOOD

A sustainable material made from post-consumer plastics, offering exceptional resistance to the elements for outdoor and indoor use.

HARDWOOD

Premium wood from dicot trees found in temperate and tropical forests, prized for its natural beauty, strength, and longevity.

CHIPBOARD

Versatile sheets made from compressed wood chips and resin, often coated or veneered, commonly used in furniture and construction.

HPL

High-Pressure Laminate used as a durable and decorative surface material for furniture and wall paneling, available in various finishes.

NATURAL VENEER

Thin slices of real wood glued onto core panels to produce beautiful and authentic flat surfaces for doors, cabinets, and furniture.
