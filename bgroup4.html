Our Core Products

We supply a curated range of high-quality engineered and natural wood products for global markets.

MDF

Medium-density fibreboard, an engineered wood product made by breaking down wood residuals into fibres, combined with wax and a resin binder.

HDF

High-density fiberboard (Hardboard) is an engineered wood product known for its strength, durability, and smooth surface.

Polywood

Made from thermoformed plastics and UV-inhibited pigments, derived mostly from post-consumer bottle waste. Durable and sustainable.

Hardwood

Premium wood from dicot trees, found in broad-leaved temperate and tropical forests, known for its beauty and longevity.

Chipboard

Rigid sheets from compressed wood chips and resin, often coated or veneered, used in furniture and construction.

HPL & Natural Veneer

High-Pressure Laminates and thin slices of natural wood used for decorative surfaces on doors, cabinets, and furniture panels.

About Bozorgi Group

Over a century of experience, driven by values and a vision for global trade.

Bozorgi Building Material Company, with proprietary brand BOZORGI GROUP, has operated as a premier trading company since 1988 under the motto “Business Without Boundary“. Our operations span the globe, covering the supply, import, export, and distribution of authorized goods.

With decades of successful experience, Bozorgi Group now focuses on supplying premium building materials, leveraging strong relationships with refineries and petrochemicals to meet customer demands. Our competitive advantage lies in our customer-centric approach, building win-win relationships founded on trust and rapid response.

Our Vision

To be a leading business and trading company in Building Materials by maintaining our values of honesty and transparency on a global scale.

Our Mission

To supply building materials with the best services and prices, satisfying our customers and expanding our business reach through experienced, modern management.
