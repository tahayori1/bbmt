About Bozorgi Group

A Legacy of Trust and Global Trade Since 1988.

Our Introduction

Since 1988, Bozorgi Group has operated under the motto “Business Without Boundary.“ We began as a trading company and have grown into a focused supplier of premier building materials, serving loyal customers across the globe.

Our competitive advantage lies in our customer-centric approach, building win-win relationships, and leveraging a vast network of suppliers to meet demands promptly and efficiently.

Our Vision

To be a leading business and trading company in building materials, upholding our core values of honesty and transparency on both national and international fronts.

Our Mission

To supply premium building materials with the best service and pricing, expanding our geographical reach through experienced teams and modern international business systems to ensure customer satisfaction.

Our Values

Honesty, loyalty, courtesy, clarity, mutual interest, and a commitment to humanity. Our most valuable capital is the satisfaction and trust of our customers and stakeholders.

Our Products

A Curated Selection of High-Quality Building Materials.

MDF

Engineered wood product made from broken down hardwood or softwood residuals, wax, and a resin binder.

HDF

High-density fiberboard (hardboard), an engineered wood product known for its strength and durability.

PLYWOOD

Made from proprietary blends of thermoformed plastics, often derived from post-consumer bottle waste.

HARDWOOD

Wood from dicot trees, typically found in broad-leaved temperate and tropical forests. Known for its density.

CHIPBOARD

Rigid sheets from compressed wood chips and resin, often coated or veneered for furniture and building.

HPL

High-pressure decorative laminates used as furniture surface materials or wall paneling for a durable finish.

NATURAL VENEER

Thin slices of wood glued onto core panels to produce flat panels for doors, cabinets, and furniture.
